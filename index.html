Uncommon Core

Uncommon Core is the AI solution our educational system needs in America. We use machine learning networks to create and grade schoolwork packets for math students at the elementary school level, all while delivering a clean and easy interface for parents and teachers to keep track of their kids. Let Uncommon Core do the grunt work so you can focus on teaching concepts. Build love and confidence in the classroom and let us handle the menial details.

Square1

Square1 is the first-ever PT modality that acts as a complete system for the neuromechanical system of the human body. Our proprietary method assesses each client's body in real time; a simple interview to identify what the body needs at that exact moment, then feeds the appropriate neuronal intervention to correct it. To learn more please get in touch with us on our website below!
